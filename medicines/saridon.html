Saridon

Saridon combines paracetamol, propyphenazone, and caffeine to relieve pain and reduce fever. Adults can take 1-2 tablets every 4-6 hours, with a maximum of 6 tablets per day. It can be taken with or without food, but avoid alcohol and other medications containing paracetamol.
