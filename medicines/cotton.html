Cotton


Indications


Used primarily for wound care, where it serves as a soft and absorbent material to gently clean and dress wounds. Additionally, it can be used for applying and removing topical medications, as well as for padding and protection in splinting and casting procedures. It is also an essential component in various diagnostic procedures, such as swabbing for sample collection and cleaning delicate instruments. Its versatility extends to personal hygiene applications, including makeup removal and baby care. Renowned for its gentle texture and high absorbency, cotton remains a fundamental resource in both medical and everyday settings.
