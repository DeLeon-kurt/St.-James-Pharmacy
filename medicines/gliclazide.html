Gliclazide 60mg, 80mg

Gliclazide is an oral medication used to control blood sugar levels in people with type 2 diabetes. It helps increase insulin production from the pancreas. For the 60 mg modified-release tablets, the usual dose is 30-120 mg once daily with breakfast. For the 80 mg tablets, the usual dose is 40-320 mg per day, divided into 1-2 doses, usually taken with meals. It is important to monitor blood sugar levels regularly and avoid skipping meals to prevent low blood sugar.
