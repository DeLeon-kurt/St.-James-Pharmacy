Amlodipine 10mg, 20mg

Amlodipine is a calcium channel blocker used to treat high blood pressure and angina (chest pain). The typical starting dose for adults is 5 mg once daily, which may be increased to a maximum of 10 mg once daily, depending on the patient's response. It can be taken with or without food. Avoid using it with large quantities of grapefruit juice, as this can affect drug levels in the blood.
