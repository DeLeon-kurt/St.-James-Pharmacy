Gauze


Indications


Gauze pads are sterile, absorbent pads used in wound care management. They are typically made of cotton or a blend of cotton and polyester. These pads are designed to gently cover and protect wounds, providing a barrier against contaminants and promoting healing. Gauze pads are available in various sizes to accommodate different wound types and can be used for minor cuts, scrapes, abrasions, and post-surgical incisions. They are an essential component of any first aid kit or medical facility, offering versatility and effectiveness in wound care protocols.
