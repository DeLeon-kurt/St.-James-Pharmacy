Betadine

Betadine is a topical antiseptic solution used to clean and disinfect wounds, cuts, and skin infections. It contains the active ingredient povidone-iodine, which kills bacteria, viruses, and fungi on the skin. To use Betadine, clean the affected area with soap and water, then apply the solution directly or with a sterile cotton swab. Let it dry before covering with a bandage or dressing. It may cause temporary skin staining. Avoid using Betadine if you are allergic to iodine or have thyroid problems. Consult a healthcare professional if the wound shows signs of infection or does not heal properly.
