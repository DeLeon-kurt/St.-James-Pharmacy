Losartan 50mg, 100mg

Losartan is an angiotensin II receptor blocker (ARB) used to treat high blood pressure and protect the kidneys in people with type 2 diabetes. The typical starting dose is 50 mg once daily, which can be increased to 100 mg once daily based on blood pressure response. It can be taken with or without food. It's important to stay hydrated and monitor kidney function and potassium levels, as Losartan can affect these.
