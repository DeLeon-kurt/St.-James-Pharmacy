ABOUT

Originally, our family was involved in the rice trade business, but my journey took a turn upon earning a pharmacy license.

Following graduation, I pursued a career as a QC (Quality Control) analyst in a manufacturing firm without giving any thought to opening a drugstore. However, after nearly two decades of nurturing a family and home, the entrepreneurial spirit reignited.

With a purposeful vision and a wealth of professional expertise, St. James Pharmacy was established.

With four years of successful operation and counting, St. James Pharmacy remains dedicated to providing our community with pharmaceutical care and services.

- St. James Pharmacy Founder
